My Favourite Bands

Daryl Hall and John Oats

Daryl Hall and John Oates are an iconic American pop-rock duo from Philadelphia known for their catchy blend of rock, soul, and pop music. Rising to fame in the 1970s and 1980s, they produced hits like "Rich Girl," "Maneater," and "You Make My Dreams," becoming best-selling artists.

Queen

Queen is a legendary British rock band formed in 1970, known for their innovative sound and electrifying performances. Led by iconic frontman Freddie Mercury, they produced timeless hits like "Bohemian Rhapsody," "We Will Rock You," and "We Are the Champions," blending rock, opera, and theatrical elements.

The XX

The xx is a British indie pop band formed in 2005, known for their minimalist, atmospheric sound that blends indie rock, electronica, and R&B. With soft, ' haunting vocals by Romy Madley Croft and Oliver Sim, and Jamie xxs unique production, they rose to fame with hits like "Crystalised" and "Angels.
