Sudoku

About Sudoku

Sudoku is a popular logic-based number puzzle game. The objective is to fill a 9x9 grid with digits from 1 to 9, ensuring that each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid contains each digit exactly once. Sudoku puzzles range from easy to extremely challenging, promoting problem-solving skills and concentration.

Sudoku techniques

X-Wing Technique

The X-Wing technique is a basic strategy used to solve Sudoku puzzles. It involves identifying two rows or columns that each contain two instances of a particular number. If the two rows or columns are aligned in such a way that the two instances of the number form the corners of a rectangle, then the number can be eliminated from the intersecting row or column.

Naked Pairs

Naked pairs are a common solving technique in Sudoku puzzles. When two cells in a row, column, or block contain only the same two numbers as candidates, they form a naked pair. This means that the two numbers can only be placed in those two cells, allowing other candidates to be eliminated from those cells.
