About Badminton

As a kid, I loved playing badminton in our backyard. Armed with a small racket and fierce determination, I'd challenge my friends and siblings to intense matches. The shuttlecock would soar high, and I'd leap to meet it, often missing but laughing every time. Sometimes the wind would send it spinning off course, but that just added to the fun. I'd cheer every successful shot, imagining I was a champion. Even though my technique was all over the place, I never tired of playing, running, and laughing. Those sunny afternoons taught me the joy of friendly competition and endless play.
