Sports I Play

Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport played by either two players (singles) or two pairs (doubles) on a rectangular court divided by a net. The objective is to hit a shuttlecock over the net, aiming to land it within the opponent's court while preventing them from returning it. Points are scored when the opposing side fails to return the shuttlecock within bounds. Known for its quick reflexes and agility, badminton requires precision, strategy, and endurance. It is widely played recreationally and competitively worldwide, including in the Olympics.

Swimming

Swimming is a popular water sport that involves moving through water using various techniques, including freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, and butterfly. It enhances cardiovascular fitness, muscle strength, and flexibility. Swimming can be enjoyed recreationally in pools, lakes, and oceans, and is also a competitive sport featured in the Olympics

Football

Football, known as soccer in some countries, is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each. The objective is to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net using any part of the body except the arms and hands (goalkeepers are an exception). Played on a rectangular field, football is known for its global appeal, with professional leagues, international tournaments like the FIFA World Cup, and passionate fan bases. It promotes teamwork, strategy, and physical fitness, making it one of the most popular sports worldwide.
