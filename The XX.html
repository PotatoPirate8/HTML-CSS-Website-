The XX

About The XX

The xx is a British indie pop band known for their minimalist and atmospheric sound, blending elements of indie rock, electronic, and R&B. Formed in 2005, the group consists of Romy Madley Croft, Oliver Sim, and Jamie Smith (known as Jamie xx). They rose to prominence with their 2009 debut album xx, which earned critical acclaim for its intimate lyrics, hushed vocals, and distinctive use of space and texture. Their music often revolves around themes of love, longing, and personal introspection, marked by a subtle, stripped-down production style that has become their signature.

Their Music

Intro

"Intro" is a song by English indie pop band The xx. It was released on 2 August 2009 as the album's opening track. The song is an instrumental track that incorporates a keyboard riff, a bass guitar, and a bass drum. The song was used in the 2010 comedy film Project X and in the 2012 film The Great Gatsby.

Angels

"Angels" is a song by English indie pop band The xx. It was released on 17 July 2012 as the album's lead single. The song is a ballad that features a guitar riff, a bass guitar, and a bass drum. The song was used in the 2012 film The Great Gatsby.
