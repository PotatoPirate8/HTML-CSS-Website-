Video Games

Noita

Noita is a magical action roguelite set in a world where every pixel is physically simulated. Fight, explore, melt, burn, freeze and evaporate your way through the procedurally generated world using spells you've crafted yourself. Explore a variety of environments ranging from coal mines to freezing wastelands while delving deeper in search for unknown mysteries.

Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is a free-to-play, 2D platform fighter by Blue Mammoth Games. Players control "Legends" with unique weapons, aiming to knock opponents off the stage. It features simple controls, various game modes, cross-platform play, and regular updates, drawing fans of competitive and casual fighting games alike.

Tetris

Tetris is a classic puzzle game where players rotate and arrange falling blocks, called Tetriminos, to form complete lines that then disappear. Developed by Alexey Pajitnov in 1984, it challenges spatial skills and quick thinking. The games simple yet addictive gameplay has made it a timeless favorite worldwide.
