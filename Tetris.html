Tetris

About Tetris

Tetris is a classic puzzle game originally developed by Alexey Pajitnov in 1984. Players arrange falling tetrominoes—geometric shapes made of four blocks—into complete horizontal lines to clear them and score points. As the game progresses, the speed of the falling pieces increases, making it more challenging. Known for its simple yet addictive gameplay, Tetris has been released on countless platforms and remains one of the most iconic and widely played games in history.

Tetris Top Players

Joseph Saelee

Joseph Saelee is a legendary Tetris player known for revolutionizing the competitive scene. He gained fame by defeating seven-time champion Jonas Neubauer to win the 2018 Classic Tetris World Championship (CTWC) at just 16 years old, marking a new era for younger players. Saelee went on to win the CTWC again in 2019 and has been recognized for his exceptional skills, particularly his hypertapping technique. Though he briefly retired, his impact on the game remains significant

Firestorm

Firestorm (Matthew McCoig) is a highly skilled Tetris player, particularly renowned in modern Tetris formats like TETR.IO and Jstris. Known for an aggressive, back-to-back gameplay style, he has dominated the competitive scene, winning numerous tournaments, including the Jstris Cups and other major events. Firestorm was considered the top player globally from 2019 to 2020 and continues to be a prominent figure in the modern Tetris community, known for his speed and precision.
