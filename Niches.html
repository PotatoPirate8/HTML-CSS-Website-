Sudoku

Sudoku is a popular logic-based number puzzle game. The objective is to fill a 9x9 grid with digits from 1 to 9, ensuring that each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid contains each digit exactly once. Sudoku puzzles range from easy to extremely challenging, promoting problem-solving skills and concentration.

Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a classic puzzle game where players uncover squares on a grid to avoid hidden mines. Each uncovered square shows a number indicating how many adjacent squares contain mines. Players use logic to mark or avoid mines, aiming to clear the board without detonating any. It's a game of strategy and caution.

Chess

Chess is a strategic board game for two players, played on an 8x8 grid with 16 pieces per player: one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king, putting it in a position where it cannot escape capture. Each piece has unique movement abilities, requiring players to think ahead, anticipate their opponent's moves, and plan complex strategies.
