About Swimming

As a kid, swimming was my favorite summer adventure. I'd jump into the pool with a splash, racing my friends to the other side, our laughter echoing across the water. I'd pretend I was a fish, gliding beneath the surface, holding my breath as long as I could. Sometimes, I'd practice different strokes, imagining I was in a big competition. The water felt freeing, refreshing, and the pool became my playground. By the end of the day, I'd be happily tired, skin wrinkled, and heart full from the fun. Those summer swims were pure magic, a mix of freedom and endless joy.

Swimmers

Micheal Phelps

Michael Phelps is an American former competitive swimmer and the most successful and most decorated Olympian of all time, with a total of 28 medals. Phelps also holds the all-time records for Olympic gold medals (23), Olympic gold medals in individual events (13), and Olympic medals in individual events (16).

Katie Ledecky

Katie Ledecky is an American competitive swimmer who has won multiple Olympic gold medals and set numerous world records. Known for her freestyle events, Ledecky has dominated the sport with her speed, endurance, and technique. She is considered one of the greatest swimmers of all time.
