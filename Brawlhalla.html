Brawlhalla

About Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is a free-to-play platform fighting game developed by Blue Mammoth Games and published by Ubisoft. Players choose from a roster of diverse characters, called Legends, each with unique weapons and abilities, and battle across various stages. The gameplay emphasizes fast-paced combat, with mechanics inspired by traditional fighting games and platformers. It supports multiple modes, including 1v1, free-for-all, and ranked matches, both online and offline.

Brawlhalla Top Players

Pavelski

Pavelski (Pavel Milev) is a professional Brawlhalla player from Bulgaria, widely recognized for his success in the competitive scene. He won the 2020 Brawlhalla World Championship (EU) and has consistently ranked high in various tournaments, including the Spring and Parsec Invitationals. Known for his aggressive playstyle and mastery of multiple Legends, Pavelski is also a content creator, sharing gameplay and strategies through platforms like YouTube and Twitch.

Sandstorm

Sandstorm (Stephen Myers) is widely regarded as one of the greatest Brawlhalla players of all time. He is a three-time World Champion, dominating both 1v1 and 2v2 tournaments. Sandstorm was the first player to reach 3000 ELO in Ranked 1v1 and has amassed over $200,000 in career earnings. Known for his mastery of the scythe and gauntlets, he has held the top rank in North America for several years. Despite retiring briefly in 2022, he later announced a return to competitive play.
